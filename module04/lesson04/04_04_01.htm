Electronic Warehouses

Where do you put the stuff you don't necessarily need but don't want to get rid of? In storage. While closets and garages are great for storing things like extra clothing and old toys, electronic databases are perfect for storing collected data.



After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • define database
  • interpret database queries
  • define mail merge
  • describe the required database fields for mail merge
  • create a database form for mail merge
