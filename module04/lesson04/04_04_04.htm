Playing the Field

Databases are similar to pizza toppings. Just like you can customize a standard cheese pizza with different toppings, you can customize a form letter by using different fields, or pieces of information, in a database. Look below to see how database fields are used to personalize standard documents.

Progress Report

Progress Report

A teacher sending a progress report would need these fields:

  • student name
  • guardian name
  • guardian address
  • subject
  • numeric grade
Bill/Invoice

Bill/Invoice

A business sending a bill or invoice would need these fields:

  • customer name
  • customer address
  • payment due date
  • amount due
  • item or service purchased
Admission Letter

Admission Letter

A college notifying a student of admission would need these fields:

  • student name
  • student address
  • date of new student orientation
  • location of new student orientation
Newsletter

Newsletter

A business, club, or organization sending monthly newsletters would need these fields:

  • recipient name
  • recipient address
  • upcoming meeting dates

The fields you include in your database depend upon your purpose.
