Managing Data

Dealing with a large amount of data can be daunting. However, Excel has tools to help you organize, sort, filter, and analyze any amount of data.

Using Tables

In 2014-2015, the Pew Research Center conducted a survey of 1,060 teens ages 13-17 regarding social media use. Imagine dealing with the data on 1,060 individual pieces of paper. Scary! Luckily, Excel allows you to create tables to manage your data on one spreadsheet.
