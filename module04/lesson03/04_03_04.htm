A Picture is Worth a Thousand Words

When is the last time you used an emoji in a text message or posted a picture to social media? Images help us express our ideas, and they appeal to our audience. After all, a happy face is the same in every language and makes us smile, too. Excel not only organizes data, it can turn your data into a cool graphic.

Fun Fact

Some artists are creating artwork with a very unlikely medium—an Excel spreadsheet. Google Tatsuo Horiuchi and his works Cherry Blossoms at Jogo Castle and Kegon Falls. Prepare to be amazed!
