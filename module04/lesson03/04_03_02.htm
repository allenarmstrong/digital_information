Excel Your Data

One way to maximize Excel's potential is to use the features on the Insert Ribbon, which can turbo-charge your data.

Functions lets you choose a predefined formula, so Excel can do the math for you.

Tables allows you to format your spreadsheet in a table to easily sort, filter, and calculate your data. You can also create an online survey.

Charts allows you to turn your data into various types of charts or graphs.

Links hyperlinks your selected data to a new webpage, picture, page, or email address.
