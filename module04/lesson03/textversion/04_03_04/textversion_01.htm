Pie Chart—Text Version

Here's how to create a pie chart in Excel Online.

Hide any data you don't want included in your chart.

Now select all the data you want represented–or this example, A2 through B10.

To create a pie chart, insert a 2-D Pie Chart from the Insert tab.

Label your pie sections by selecting Data Labels from the Chart Ribbon, then Inside End.

You can move your Legend to wherever works best for reading your chart.
