Excel Your Data—Text Version

Functions let you choose a predefined formula, so Excel can do the math for you.

Tables allow you to format your spreadsheet in a table to easily sort, filter, and calculate your data. You can also create an online survey.

Charts allow you to turn your data into various types of charts or graphs.

Links hyperlink your selected data to a new webpage, picture, page, or email address.

Comments allow you to add notes to the spreadsheet.To view your comments, open the Review Ribbon and select "Show Comments."
