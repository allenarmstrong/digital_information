Sort it Out

Pop quiz: What is the most common way to sort information?

  1. Alphabet
  2. Color
  3. Location
  4. Size

If you answered "Alphabet," you get a gold star. Alphabetical order makes it easy to find information you need, which is why dictionaries, phone books, class rosters, works cited pages, and even your iTunes, music are arranged in A, B, C … order.


Excel allows you to sort the data in your spreadsheet in two ways: ascending order (A, B, C …) or descending order (Z, Y, X …). In fact, this method of sorting information is so commonly used that you can access it in the following places:

  • Home Ribbon
  • Data Ribbon
  • Table Drop-Down Arrow
