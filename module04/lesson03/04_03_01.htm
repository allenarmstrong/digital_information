Data Delivery

Data brings to mind facts, figures, statistics, people in white lab coats, gray-haired professors—nothing terribly exciting. But data is powerful. Data influences elections, determines pay raises, settles court cases, and so much more. Excel has tools to organize your data, help you analyze it, and then present it in the most compelling way.



After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • define charts and tables
  • identify various types of charts and graphs
  • organize and analyze data on a worksheet
  • interpret data on commonly used charts, graphs, and tables
