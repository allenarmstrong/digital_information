Formatting, Formulas, and Functions

Sometimes, when things come together, magic happens. Think about cookie dough ice cream, smart phones, s'mores, and Snuggies. (Well, not so much the last one, but you get the idea.) Excel combines an electronic spreadsheet and a giant calculator into one, so you can organize, analyze, and calculate data.



After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • format a worksheet by manipulating views
  • identify features of the Excel View Ribbon
  • define key terms related to formulas and functions
  • apply formulas and functions
