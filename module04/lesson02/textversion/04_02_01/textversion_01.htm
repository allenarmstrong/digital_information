Change Your Perspective—Text Version

  1. Editing View allows you to actively edit your worksheet. It is the default view in Excel Online.
  2. Reading View allows you to view and search your worksheet, but you are unable to edit in this view.
  3. Headings allows you see the default column headings (letters across the top of the worksheet) and row headings (numbers down the left side of the worksheet). You can manually enter column and row headings by selecting the cell and typing in your desired heading. To hide the default headings in a worksheet, uncheck Headings.
  4. Gridlines allow you to see the lightly colored lines that appear around cells. They outline cells, columns, and rows. To hide the gridlines in a worksheet, uncheck Gridlines.
