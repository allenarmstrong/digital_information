Creating a Spreadsheet

When you select the Excel app in Office Online, you can create a new blank workbook or choose from various pre-styled templates. Once you have a blank worksheet, you are ready to fill in the data. To do this, select the "A1" cell and begin typing. You can use your mouse or the keyboard arrows to move up, down, and across. To move down one cell, select "Enter" on your keyboard. To move right one cell, select "Tab" on your keyboard.
