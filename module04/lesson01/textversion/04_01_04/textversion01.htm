Home Sweet Home—Text Version

  1. Undo allows you to undo or reverse your most recent actions. If you change your mind, simply select "redo" and what you undid will reappear.
  2. Clipboard allows you to copy, cut, and paste text on the document.
  3. Font allows you to customize your data to give it the exact look you want. You can change the style, size, and color of a text. You can also bold, italicize, underline, double underline, and highlight text.
  4. Alignment allows you to align text in the top, middle, or bottom of the cell; align text to the left, center, or right in the cell; wrap text so all content is visible in the cell; and merge cells into one larger cell.
  5. Number allows you to change the format of numbers in your cells (numbers, percentage, currency, date, time, etc.).
  6. Tables allows you to format your data as a table to easily organize, analyze, sort, and filter the data.
  7. Cells allows you to insert a new cell or delete a cell.
  8. Editing allows you to AutoSum (the ability to total a column or row of numbers by selecting the cells you want to sum), clear cells, sort cells in ascending (A-Z)/descending (Z-A) order, and find specific content.
