Formatting Data in Excel—Text Version

Formatting your data in Excel makes it easier to read and understand.

To begin, start by selecting the cell, column, row, or even range of cells that you want to format.

Once selected, you can format the cells in any way you want, including modifying the font, changing the number formatting, and even modifying the number of decimal places displayed.

To make sure all your content is visible in the cell, adjust the column width or row height as needed.
