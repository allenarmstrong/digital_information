Interview Questions

Here are the most common interview questions:

  1. Tell me about yourself.
  2. Why are you interested in working here/doing this job?
  3. What do you know about this company/job?
  4. What makes you qualified for this job?
  5. What are your strengths?
  6. What are your weaknesses?
  7. Why should we hire you?
  8. What kind of experience do you have?
  9. How do you handle conflict?
  10. Tell me about a time you and a co-worker disagreed.
  11. How would others describe you?
  12. How do you handle criticism?
  13. Why did you leave your last job?
  14. Where do you see yourself in five years?
  15. Do you have questions for me?
