Cover Story

A cover letter is a letter that you submit to a potential employer with your résumé. It gives more information about who you are and what you can do for a company. So why doesn't a résumé speak for itself? Honestly, because it's kind of boring. Résumés contain the essential elements, which are important, but they don't allow you to be creative. A good cover letter is a way to express yourself, showcase your writing, and give a sense of your personality.
