Show Off

There aren't many times when you're encouraged to be a show off, but when applying for a job, go for it! There are several opportunities during the job-seeking process for you to showcase your skills, so promote yourself.


After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • explain key aspects of job-seeking steps, including application, cover letter, résumé, interview, and follow-up
  • demonstrate job-seeking skills required for entry-level employment
