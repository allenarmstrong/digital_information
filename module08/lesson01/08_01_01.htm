Your Future in the Workforce

Watson, the computer built by IBM, beat the all-time human Jeopardy champion and two teams from MIT and Harvard. Why should this matter to you? In addition to replacing jobs like factory work, technology is also eliminating higher-skilled jobs. What does this mean for you? To make sure you don't become "jobsolete"—meaning replaceable by technology—you need to have the right skills to land a career in a growing industry.


After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • describe the potential impact of local and global trends on career plans and life goals
  • distinguish between soft and hard skills
  • analyze personal skills and abilities in comparison with various business-related jobs and careers
  • identify specific job requirements in the workforce
