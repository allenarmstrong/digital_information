IT's Trendy

Information technology (IT) is the industry that keeps the world's economy running. Without IT, banks, corporations, education, healthcare, governments, and media could not thrive. Careers in computers and IT are also expected to grow 13% between now and 2024. So if job security is important to you, IT has a strong outlook.
