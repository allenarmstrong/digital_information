Tools of the Trade

You can't build a house without the right tools, nor can you make the most of your career without the right resources. Whether you're trying to figure out your future career path or ways to advance in your current job, you need a toolkit of resources. This lesson will help you begin building your collection.


After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use career resources
  • create and implement a plan to facilitate career growth and gain job skills
  • describe the importance of professional mentor relationships
