What Is It?

A software that protects your computer by:

  • Searching for malicious files such as viruses, Trojan horses, and worms
  • Preventing spyware, adware, and other malware
  • Removing and destroying harmful files

Types

  • Anti-Virus – Protects against computer viruses that damage the computer or interfere with normal operations
  • Anti-Spyware – Protects against software that collects personal data from your computer without your knowledge
  • Firewall – Protects from threats that access your computer over the Internet

Free vs. Paid

  • Free – Scaled-back version providing adequate protection against common invaders
  • Paid – Detects new versions of existing virus code and offers technical support

What Best Meets Your Needs?

Research reviews, ask an expert, or use a trial subscription to help you choose the best option for you.

Standalone Product

  • More advanced
  • Research and customize your security system

Security Suite

  • Easier to use
  • Wider range of protection
  • Tends to slow down computers
  • Not as thorough as standalone products

Built-in Firewalls

  • Found in Windows and Mac OSX
  • Must turn them on

Web Browser Security Settings

  • Limited protection provided
  • Must customize and disable features

Slide 3

Get the Most from Your Security

  • Turn on automatic update.
  • Renew subscriptions.
  • Always enable security software.
  • Restart your computer at least once a week.
  • Uninstall all anti-virus programs if you decide to change products.
  • Install software updates to fix vulnerabilities.
  • Use technical support with difficult problems.
  • Download from trusted sites.
