Computer Parts and Pieces

Computers are a sum of their parts: there's the hardware—the physical aspects of the computer—and the software—that's everything that's going on inside that we can't "touch." The way these pieces and parts work together is what makes computers so powerful.



After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • understand the pieces and parts of a computer
  • identify the different categories of peripherals
  • explain how various peripherals are used
  • explain the history of operating systems
  • explain the purpose of various operating systems
