Design Tab—Text Version

Themes allows you to choose slides with preformatted colors and designs. The color and designs are applied to each slide (title, content, section header, etc.) in your presentation.

Variants allows you to choose an alternative color palette for your selected theme. The color palette is predefined based on color theory.

Customize allows you to format the background (the area behind your content) with a solid color or a picture saved on your computer. You can customize either the background of individual slides or all the slides in your presentation by selecting "Apply to All."
