07.03 Presenting Color Theory Rubric

(30 points possible)

Criteria Excellent
6-5 points		 Needs Improvement
4-0 points		 Score Yourself
Slides A total of four slides are used, and the appropriate layout is applied. Three or fewer slides are used, and/or the appropriate layout is not applied.  
Color Combination Color combination is visually appealing. Color combination is not visually appealing.  
Explanation Explanation of the mood and the effectiveness of the color combination is specific and accurate. Explanation of the mood and the effectiveness of the color combination is not specific and/or is not accurate.  
Contrast Contrast between the background and font color makes the text easy to read. Contrast between the background and font color does not make the text easy to read.  
Mechanics Proper spelling, grammar, and punctuation are used throughout. Proper spelling, grammar, and/or punctuation are not used throughout.
