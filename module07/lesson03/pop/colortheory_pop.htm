07.03 Presenting Color Theory Assignment

Directions

For this project, you will create four slides that use effective color combinations to convey mood and add visual appeal.


Step One: Planning

Take some time to think about how colors express certain moods and feelings. You can research the mood of your favorite colors by doing a Google search for "psychology of color" to help you get started. When you brainstorm the "Real Life Examples of Color Choices," look around you, think of the places you shop, take a walk outside, open a magazine, or close your eyes and daydream. Complementary colors and purposeful color combinations are all around you.

Step Two: Present Your Colors

Follow these guidelines to properly format your assignment:

  • Use a Title and Content layout for each slide.
  • Title each slide with your selected combination of background color and text color (For example: Purple and yellow).
  • In your own words, give a brief explanation on each slide of the mood your color combination conveys and why the colors work well together.
  • Use a high degree of contrast, so your text is easily readable.
  • Use correct spelling, grammar, and punctuation. (Don't forget PowerPoint has spell check.)

Step Three: Save

Save your PowerPoint presentation to your OneDrive using the file name format, "LastName_FirstName_M7L3." For example, if your name is Jane Smith, you would save the file as "Smith_Jane_M7L3."
