Motion Effects

Did you know your PowerPoint slides can come to life? Not in a Dr. Frankenstein kind of way but through automated transitions and animations. However, to avoid overkill, transitions and animations should be used cautiously. (Okay, that's it for the life and death puns.)



After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • apply transitions to individual PowerPoint slides and all slides
  • apply animations to PowerPoint objects
