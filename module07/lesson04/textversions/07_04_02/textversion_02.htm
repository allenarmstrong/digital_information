Transitions—Text Version

Slide transitions add an animation-like effect to your slides.

To add, start by selecting a transition type from the Transition Ribbon. By default, the slide transition will be applied to the current slide, but you can also choose to apply to all.

View the effect of the transition by selecting "Slide Show" from the View Ribbon.

Advance through the slideshow by selecting the "right arrow." The slides will appear using the chosen transition.
