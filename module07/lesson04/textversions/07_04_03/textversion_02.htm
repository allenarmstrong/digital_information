Animations—Text Version

To add some life to your PowerPoint presentations, check out the Animations feature.

Start by selecting the slide and content you want to animate.

Then choose the type of animation you want to apply from the Animation Ribbon.

To see how it looks, select "Slide Show" from the View Ribbon.

Advance through the slideshow and animation using the right arrow. Your animated items will appear one at a time as the arrow is selected.
