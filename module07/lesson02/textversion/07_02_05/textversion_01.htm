Insert SmartArt—Text Version

SmartArt is visual graphics that can add new dimension to your presentation.

To begin, select "SmartArt" from the Insert Ribbon.

Now, find the SmartArt that best fits your needs.

Select [click on] the graphic to add text as you want it to appear on the slide.

You can format your SmartArt by changing things like text alignment, style, or color from the Design Ribbon.
