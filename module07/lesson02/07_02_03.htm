The Power of Pictures

Brain science shows that visual images are easier to process than text, but pictures are also more engaging. When you combine strong content and graphical elements, you have a recipe for success, which is why they are used together so often in social media, advertisements, web pages, magazines, and more.

Look at this student's PowerPoint slides on the dangers of distracted driving. The image sets the tone for the slide, supports the statistic, and engages the audience more than text alone.
