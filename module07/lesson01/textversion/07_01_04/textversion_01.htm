PowerPoint Home Ribbon—Text Version

  1. Undo allows you to undo or reverse your most recent action. If you change your mind, simply select "redo" and what you undid will reappear.
  2. Clipboard allows you to copy, cut, and paste text on the document. The format painter allows you to copy the formatting of text and apply it to text in other locations.
  3. Delete allows you to delete an entire slide.
  4. Slides allows you to create and insert new slides, change the layout of existing slides, duplicate a slide, and hide certain slides.
  5. Font allows you to adjust the font style, size, and color of text and bold, italicize, and underline text on a slide.
  6. Paragraph allows you to create bulleted and numbered lists; increase or decrease the indent of an entire paragraph; align text to the left, right, or center; and modify line spacing.
  7. Drawing allows you to add shapes, arrange and order objects, use quick styles (preformatted colors and shapes), fill background color, outline shapes, and duplicate slides.
