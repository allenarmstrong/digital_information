Powerful Presentations

If the idea of presenting to a room full of people makes your knees shake, you're not alone. Review this slideshow to glimpse how PowerPoint can help.




After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • define key PowerPoint terms and features
  • describe the Home and View Ribbon features
  • create a new PowerPoint presentation and open existing ones
  • describe different slide layouts and designs
  • insert title and content slides
  • adjust content views
