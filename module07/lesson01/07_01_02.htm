Make a Statement

PowerPoint. Power. Point. The key to an effective presentation is to wave your hands above your head to get your audience's attention. (Not really. But surprising your audience is a good engagement technique.) The real recipe is to be clear and connect to your audience in some way. Combining interesting text, a purposeful layout, an appealing design, and an exciting image on each slide will make a bold statement and interest audiences.

First Things First

When you open the PowerPoint Online app, select "New blank presentation" to create a brand-new presentation, or select the "See more templates" link and choose an appropriate design template. Using a template is like using a cookie cutter. You get a unique design without having to start from scratch each time you create a new presentation.
