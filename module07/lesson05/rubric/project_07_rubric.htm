07.05 Module Seven Project Rubric

(100 points possible)

Criteria Excellent
10–7 points		 Needs Improvement
6–0 points		 Score Yourself
Overall Presentation Presentation contains the required content. Presentation does not contain the required content.  
Theme A theme appropriate to the overall mood and tone of the presentation is applied to all slides. The theme is not appropriate to the overall mood and tone of the presentation and/or is not applied to all slides.  
Slides Presentation includes 12 slides total, including a title slide, 10 content slides, a conclusion slide, and four different content layouts. Presentation does not include 12 slides total and/or is missing a title slide, four different content layouts, and/or a conclusion slide.  
Bullets, Hyperlinks, and SmartArt Bullets, hyperlink, and SmartArt are used effectively and correctly. Bullets, hyperlink, and SmartArt are not used effectively and/or correctly.  
Content Content conveys meaningful reflection and is communicated effectively. Content does not convey meaningful reflection and/or is not communicated effectively.  
Graphics Four relevant images, one self-portrait, one video, and three shapes are used, and they enhance content. Four relevant images, one self-portrait, one video, and three shapes are not used, and/or they do not enhance content.  
Font and Color Font size, style, color, and contrast are easy to read and complementary. Font size, style, color, and contrast are not easy to read and/or are not complementary.  
Transitions and Animations Two transitions and two animations are used, and they emphasize content. Fewer than two transitions and animations are used, they are overused, and/or they do not emphasize content.  
Creativity Content is visually appealing, engaging, and original. Content is not visually appealing, engaging, and/or original.  
Mechanics Proper spelling, grammar, and punctuation are used throughout presentation. Proper spelling, grammar, and punctuation are not used throughout presentation.
