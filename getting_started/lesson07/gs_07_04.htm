Academic Integrity (continued)

How Do We Monitor Integrity?

Online identification tools are used to compare work submitted to information available on the Internet as well as to all prior assessments submitted by all students.

Discussion-Based Assessments are conducted routinely between the instructor and the student. These must be completed free of any distraction or influence from others. This is also a time to ask your instructor questions.

Proctored exams are a routine part of how we ensure that student learning is taking place. These include randomly selected students. Refusal to take a proctored exam at any time will result in a failing grade in the course.
