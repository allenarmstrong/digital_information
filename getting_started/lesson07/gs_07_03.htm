Academic Integrity (continued)

What are Considered Violations of Academic Integrity?
Here are Some Examples:

  • Plagiarism—defined as any submission of another's work as your own and of not citing authorship properly (see some examples of plagiarism)
  • Copying and pasting—from any Internet resource
  • Submitting another student's work as your own
  • Using answer sites
  • Using unauthorized calculators
  • Using translators for foreign language courses
  • Having someone else impersonate you
  • Not protecting your password and your work
