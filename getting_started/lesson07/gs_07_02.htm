Academic Integrity (continued)

Academic Integrity = Academic Honesty

A full commitment to academic integrity is expected from every student and parent:

  • Academic integrity is a core value all students and parents must adhere to as part of the learning process.
  • The curriculum is designed so that resources needed for student learning are provided within our coursework.
  • When referencing information in your writing, be sure to cite your sources properly.
  • Instructors are an important resource as well and should be contacted when student assistance is needed.
