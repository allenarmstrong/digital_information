Lessons & Assessments: Submitting Your Work

Submitting Your Work

As you complete each lesson, you typically will need to submit an assignment or an exam for grading.

Rubrics

Certain assignments contain a rubric that your teacher will use to grade your assignment. The rubric shows you exactly the kind of work your teacher will expect from you. Before beginning your assignment, read through the rubric to understand the expectations of the assignment. Then, before you submit your work, compare it against the rubric to make sure you're turning in the best work possible.
