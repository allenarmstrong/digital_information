Types of Assessments

Lesson Level Assignments

In these types of assignments, you will be given a grading rubric and detailed instructions on how to complete the assignment. Please make sure to check your work against the rubric before submitting to your instructor.

Module Level Projects

In these types of assignments, you will be given the overall objective and a grading rubric. To be successful, read the project directions carefully and look back on previous lessons and work in the module. Compare your finished assignment to the rubric before submitting to your instructor.
