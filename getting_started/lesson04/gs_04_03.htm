Types of Assessments (continued)

Lesson Level Quizzes, Module Exams, and Segment Exams

In these types of assessments, you will choose one correct answer from the choices given. To be successful, make sure to read each selection carefully before answering.

Discussion-Based Assessments

Discussion-Based Assessments are conducted routinely between the instructor and the student. These must be completed free of any distraction or influence from others. This is also a time to ask your instructor questions.
