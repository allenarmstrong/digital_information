Collaboration: What You Need To Know

Choose Your Collaboration

Throughout this course you will find opportunities to collaborate with your fellow classmates. View the Collaboration Presentation or read the text version to get tips and guidelines for working together.


A collaboration activity is an assignment you complete with the help of another student or group of students. You are required to complete your choice of at least one collaboration option in each segment of this course.

Synchronous Collaboration

Contact your instructor to learn about collaboration opportunities that will allow you to interact with your classmates online, in real-time, using Web 2.0 tools. If you choose to participate in the synchronous collaboration opportunity, your instructor will explain what you need to submit for grading.

Asynchronous Collaboration

In this course, you also have the opportunity to participate in an asynchronous collaboration with one of your classmates. Contact your teacher to find the best method to find a collaboration partner.


Complete the Collaboration Reflection Worksheet and submit it for grading. Your collaboration assignment will be evaluated according to the Collaboration Rubric.
