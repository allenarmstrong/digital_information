FLVS Full-Time Students

The pace options below do not apply to students enrolled in the FLVS Full-Time Program. To see your assignments and due dates for the semester, select the Calendar link in your course.

Choose a pace for completing this course from the guides below. If you wish to change pace during the progression of the course, you must have permission from your instructor, your parent(s) or guardian(s), and your guidance counselor. Think carefully about the time you have each week to devote to this class, and choose your pace wisely.

Print and fill in the appropriate pace guide and place it in your work area for future reference. Please give a copy to your parent(s) or guardian(s).

Pace Options

  • Traditional: Complete the course in 36 weeks. This pace is recommended.
  • Traditional Accelerated: Complete the course in 16–18 weeks.
  • Second Segment Only: Complete the course in one segment (16–18 weeks).
    Note: This option is for students who are only seeking credit for the second segment and did not take the first segment online with this school.
  • Extended Pace (Requires teacher, parent, and guidance counselor approval): Your individual pace will be determined by your teacher. You will be required to submit a petition for extended pace documenting approval from your parents and your school guidance counselor. When the completed petition form is received, it will be kept on file by your online instructor.

Individual Pace Planner

The Individual Pace Planner is a template that allows the teacher or student to input information to formulate a completion plan for the course. This can be based on planned hours in the course per week or start and end dates. This planner will generate a schedule that outlines what assessments should be completed each week to accomplish the goals set.
