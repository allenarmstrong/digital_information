Accepting constructive criticism can be hard, so here are some tips to make it easier:

  • Avoid becoming defensive about the criticism
  • Listen to the critic's feedback and ask questions for clarification
  • Reflect upon the feedback
  • Seek solutions to the behavior that prompted the criticism
  • Learn from the experience and move on
