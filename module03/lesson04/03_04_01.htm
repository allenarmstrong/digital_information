Project Protocol

Friends and family are typically pretty forgiving. For example, if there is a conflict over whose turn it is to unload the dishwasher, you probably won't get fired from the family for not working well as a team. In the workplace, though, working together productively and effectively is a highly valued trait in an employee.



After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • demonstrate ways of accepting constructive criticism on team projects
  • apply strategies to resolve conflicts in work situations
  • demonstrate an awareness of project management concepts
