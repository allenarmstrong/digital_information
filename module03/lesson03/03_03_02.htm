Think Bigger

Companies and organizations who want to be leaders in the global marketplace need to stay informed and up-to-date with one vast and ever-evolving aspect of life: technology. With the invention of the internet and other technologies like cell phones and tablets, the global market has expanded in unimaginable ways. But as you may already know, the possibilities are endless as emerging technologies continue to develop.

Think Smarter

Smart mobile devices combine a camera, game console, internet, music player, personal office, phone, video player, and more into one device that can be taken on the go. These devices have changed the way companies, organizations, and everyday people conduct day-to-day activities. Look at the following examples:

  • People use wearable bands to track their steps and activity levels and stream music during activities
  • Homeowners use smartphones to monitor their smart appliances, activate alarms, turn on/off lights, and manage the interior temperature when at home and/or away
  • Individuals use e-readers to download and read books, magazines, newspapers, etc.
  • Restaurants provide tablets to customers (to look up menu items and communicate with the server) and to servers (to enter orders and complete payment transactions)

One major benefit of using smart devices is the ability to mesh (connect) all the different devices you use to access applications and information or interact with people, social communities, organizations, and businesses.

Fun Fact

In a report by GE, it is proposed that intelligent machines with efficiency gains of just 1% could result in a 15-year savings of:

  • $30 billion worth of jet fuel for the airline industry
  • $63 billion in global health care savings
  • $66 billion savings in fuel consumption for the global gas-fired power plant fleet
