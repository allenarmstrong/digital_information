Technlogies - Text Version

Technologies that can be used even in a restaurant:

  • Access points throughout the restaurant provide WiFi to connect smart devices.
  • Managers can control lights, the alarm system, and even the A/C from their smartphones with the right software.
  • The greeter can input reservations and seat people, as well as communicate with servers on available tables with a tablet.
  • Smart appliances in the kitchens can indicate when food is low and needs to be restocked.
  • Tablets at each table allow customers to browse the menu and place orders without a server.
