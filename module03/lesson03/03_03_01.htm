Emerging Technologies

In the 1960's, an animated show called The Jetsons gave viewers a glimpse of the future. The show portrayed drones, holograms, jetpacks, flying cars, robotic maids, smart appliances, and smart watches. Back then, the technology depicted in the show seemed far-fetched. Now, these technologies exist in some form or another, making what seemed like science fiction a reality.



After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • compare and contrast emerging technologies
  • describe the impact of emerging technologies on the global marketplace
