Success in the Workplace

Would you like to be the next Mark Zuckerberg, founder of Facebook and, as of 2015, worth an estimated 32.5 billion dollars? If the answer is "yes," read on! Or would you like to work at a company that offers employees gourmet food for lunch, college tuition reimbursement, onsite gym and medical facilities, and pay for volunteer work? If so, keep reading! There are some essential elements that all businesses should have and all future workers should know in order to achieve success.



After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • demonstrate awareness of workplace essentials
  • describe personal and interpersonal skills necessary for the workplace
