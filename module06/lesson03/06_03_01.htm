Image Obsessed

Open Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, or almost any other website, and you will see that images rule the web. If you want to be a web design guru, you need to learn what images are most effective and how to obtain them.


After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • access and digitize graphics through various resources
  • enhance web pages through the addition of images and graphics, including animation
  • use basic functions of WYSIWYG editors
