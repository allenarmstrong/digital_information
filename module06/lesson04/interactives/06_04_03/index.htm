Flickr


Flickr

Flickr is an online social photo-sharing website that is owned and operated by Yahoo! The site alows you to create photobooks and share your photo libraries with others.


Photobucket



Photobucket

Photobucket is similar to Flickr, but is run independently, and allows you to upload videos as well as photos.




iStock


iStock

iStock is a website that specializes in stock photography and artwork that anyone creates. Others can search the photo library and pay for the use of photos at different sizes.


Thinkstock



Thinkstock

Thinkstock is a photography and artwork search engine that looks for files matching a user's search words on catalogs like iStock.
