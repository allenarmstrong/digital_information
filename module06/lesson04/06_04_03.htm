Picturing Your Web Page

Sometimes John may not have access to the graphics he wants, so utilizing online graphics is a solid choice. He learns that many graphics can be obtained from different image hosting sites, such as Flickr, Tinypic, and Photobucket. Some graphics have usage restrictions on them, so it's important he is aware of the permissions for a photo in order to protect himself from violating any rules. Stock photo sites are also great for finding images, as they cover a wide array of topics. Stock photos are professional photos of places, events, people, and nature that are purchased and sold on a royalty-free basis and can be used for commercial purposes. The downside is that in order to use photos from most stock photo sites, he has to pay for a membership or for a certain amount of photo downloads. Some of the most popular stock photo sites are ThinkStock and iStock. As John continues reading, he comes across another type of graphic: clip art. Clip art is pre-made, copyright free illustrations used to represent many different topics. They are typically included as part of a software program or available online through clip art websites.
