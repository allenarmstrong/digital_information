Color Matters

It's one thing to tell you that colors are powerful, but it's another to show you. So here's some proof: Freshly cut meat can begin turning brown as soon as it is exposed to air. Even though it's perfectly safe, the color brown is not associated with freshness. For this reason, many meat manufacturers inject beef with carbon monoxide to give it a pink color. In fact, researchers in a study called "Impact of Color in Marketing" determined that up to 90% of snap judgments made about products can be based on color alone.

What does this mean? Color matters!
