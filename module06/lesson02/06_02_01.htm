The Art of Design

There's a reason most people don't wear plaid and leopard print together: they don't look good. The same can be said of certain color combinations and design elements. Therefore, when your goal is to make something look as visually appealing as possible, you have to be color-conscious and have an eye for design.


After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • define color theory
  • explain how color theory relates to web design
  • apply design and color theory to a web page
  • explore design and color theory to create dynamic and appealing visuals
  • describe storyboarding techniques
