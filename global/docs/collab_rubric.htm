Collaboration Rubric

(40 points possible)

Criteria Excellent Good Needs Improvement

Task, Description, Peer and Self-Evaluation

15–13 points

The student meets all of the required criteria:

Detailed task description

Ratings for team and self

Thoughtful comments on performance of team and self

Reviews consistent with evidence and project

12–9 points

The student meets most of the required criteria:

Detailed task description

Ratings for team and self

Thoughtful comments on performance of team and self

Reviews consistent with evidence and project

8–0 points

The student meets some of the required criteria:

Detailed task description

Ratings for team and self

Thoughtful comments on performance of team and self

Reviews consistent with evidence and project

Reflection

15–13 points

The work contains completely detailed contributions of self and team, insight into collaborative efforts, and specific areas for improvement.

12–9 points

The work contains mostly detailed contributions of self and team, insight into collaborative efforts, and specific areas for improvement.

8–0 points

The work contains somewhat detailed contributions of self and team, insight into collaborative efforts, and specific areas for improvement.

Evidence

10–8 points

The student meets all the following criteria:

At least one piece of evidence to demonstrate successful collaboration

Clear, easy to identify evidence

7–5 points

The student meets most of the following criteria:

At least one piece of evidence to demonstrate successful collaboration

Clear, easy to identify evidence

4–0 points

The student meets some of the following criteria:

At least one piece of evidence to demonstrate successful collaboration

Clear, easy to identify evidence
