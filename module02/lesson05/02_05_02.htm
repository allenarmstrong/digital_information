Rev Up Your Review

Having errors on a document is like having stains on your shirt. (Don't worry. It happens to everyone.) But just like you wouldn't wear a stained shirt on a first date, you don't want to send or submit a document with errors.

Fun Fact

Did you know that one misspelled word can cost a company millions of dollars? According to online entrepreneur Charles Duncombe, once a misspelling of the word "tights" was corrected on the TightsPlease.co.uk website, profits doubled. Word Online has features on the Review tab to help you catch and correct mistakes, making your document squeaky clean.

Misspelled words on a Word Online document are underlined in red. When the Spelling tool is selected, correct spelling options appear for the first misspelled word. Selecting the word's proper spelling from the menu automatically corrects the misspelled word. The Spelling tool should be used until the message stating, "Spell check complete. No mistakes were found." appears.

The New Comment tool is a resource for you, or those you share your documents with, to make notes in the margins. To use this tool, highlight the place in your document where you want to add a note, select "New Comment," and add your note.

You can access previous versions of your Word Online documents. To track your document versions, go to the OneDrive app, select the Documents folder, right-click the document you want to track, and select Version history. A list of the document versions will appear in the left margin. When you select a previous version, you can download or restore that version.
