Proofing Before Sending

Tsih leson wll hlep you meploy Wrod Onilne tloos to poorf yor wrok brefore snednig it. Yikes! There are some major issues with that first sentence. Before sending an email, submitting a project, or publishing your blog, use the resources in Word Online to spot and correct mistakes. Imagine how much clearer the first sentence would be without errors: This lesson will help you employ Word Online tools to proof your work before sending it. Now that's better.


After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • describe the Review tab features
  • use the Spelling check tool
  • use the Comments tool on a document
  • describe the concept of "tracking" document versions
