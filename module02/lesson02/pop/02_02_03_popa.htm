How to Create an Email Signature Line—Text Version

Here's how to create an email signature. First, select settings (the little wheel icon in the top right corner) and choose "Options."

From here, select "Formatting, font, and signature" under the "Writing Email" section.

Create your signature by typing in the "Personal signature" text box. It should include your name, title, company, address, and phone number when applicable. Hit "Save" after you have finished.

To view your signature, go back to Outlook and create a new email. Your new signature should now appear automatically.
