The Ins and Outs of the Inbox


Could you be the Guinness World Records holder for having the most unread emails in your inbox? These days, in addition to getting work and personal emails, people also receive a lot of spam, which makes it difficult to distinguish between important messages and junk mail. Luckily, you can reduce the spam that appears in your inbox and send it directly to the "Junk Mail" folder. You also have the ability to manage who is allowed to send you email messages. Follow the steps below to manage your settings and automatically move emails to your junk folder.

Directions

  1. Select the gear icon in the top-right corner of the email window.
  2. Scroll down and select "Options."
  3. Select the Preventing junk email option.
  4. Then, select Filters and reporting to choose a junk email filter, automatically delete junk email, report unwanted email to the sender, and block content from unknown senders.
  5. Select "Save" to save your settings.
  6. Next, select Safe and blocked senders to manage who is allowed to send you email messages.

To move a message from your inbox to another folder, drag and drop the message to the folder where you want it to be filed.

You can create new folders to suit your email organization needs. To do this, select the "New folder" option and name it.
