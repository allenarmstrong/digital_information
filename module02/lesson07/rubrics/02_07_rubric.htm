02.07 Module Two Project Rubric

(100 points possible)

Criteria Excellent
10–7 points		 Needs Improvement
6–0 points		 Score Yourself
Document Title The title is centered at the top of the document and formatted using a font style, size, and color that enhances the overall design. The title is not centered at the top of the document and/or formatted using a font style, size, and color that enhances the overall design.  
Picture A relevant picture is included on the document. The picture is not relevant or is missing from the document.  
Picture Formatting The picture has a frame around it from the picture styles. The picture frame is not from the picture styles or is missing from the document.  
Text The text requirement is met and relevant to the subject. The text requirement is not met and/or not relevant to the subject.  
Text Formatting The text is formatted with a font style, size, and color that enhances the document. The text is not formatted with a font style, size, or color that enhances the document.  
Hyperlink A hyperlink is included and relates to the subject. A hyperlink is not included and/or does not relate to the subject.  
Table or Footer The required table or footer is included and formatted properly. The required table or footer is not included and/or not formatted properly.  
Layout and Design The overall layout and design is visually appealing and supports the purpose of the document. The overall layout and design is not visually appealing and/or does not support the purpose of the document.  
Spelling Little or no spelling errors exist. Many spelling errors exist.  
Filename for Saved Document The filename for the saved document is formatted properly. The filename for the saved document is not formatted properly.
