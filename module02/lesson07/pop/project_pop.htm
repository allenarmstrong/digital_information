02.07 Module Two Project

Directions

For this project, you have three options, but you will only choose one. Each option requires you to create a new Word Online document and use the tools on the Home and Insert tabs to design and style your project. Read each option carefully, so you understand the purpose and requirements.

Option One

Write a Bio for an Online Magazine

Congratulations! You were selected as a columnist for an up-and-coming online teen magazine. You will be this month's feature writer, but before you get to work on your first article, the magazine needs you to write a short biography. Here is what your document must include:

  • A document title: Your name centered at the top of the document, formatted using a font style, size, and color that enhances the overall design
  • A picture of yourself, or a picture that represents you, with a frame around the image
  • A short paragraph describing yourself and your interests, formatted using a font style, size, and color that enhances the overall design
  • A hyperlink to a website that indicates your main interest or hobby (this must be school-appropriate)
  • A table with two rows and two columns and the following headers: For row 1, "Article Subject" and for row 2, "Article Due Date." Fill in an article subject of your choosing and a due date.
  • No spelling errors
  • A properly formatted file name for the saved document: LastName_FirstName_Bio

Option Two

Design an Event Flyer

Since you are taking Digital Information Technology, your friends nominated you to create a flyer for an upcoming event (like a party, a pep rally, a fundraiser, etc.). You are up to the challenge since you have the Word Online skills to produce documents that are fun and informative. Here is what your flyer must include:

  • A document title: The name of the event centered at the top of the document, formatted using a font style, size, and color that enhances the overall design
  • A picture related to the theme of the event with a frame around the image
  • A bulleted list of the key points of the event (items like the date, time, location, attire, etc.), using a font style, size, and color that enhances the overall design
  • A hyperlink to a website that relates to the event theme (this must be school-appropriate)
  • A footer with your name and email address centered on the bottom of the page
  • No spelling errors
  • A properly formatted file name for the saved document: LastName_FirstName_Flyer

Option Three

Create an Advertisement

What is the best invention of the 21st century? Is it the smartphone, Facebook, Taco Bell's Doritos Locos Taco? Your job is to create an advertisement for the item to prove its awesomeness. Your advertisement must include the following:

  • A document title: The name of the item centered at the top of the document, formatted using a font style, size, and color that enhances the overall design
  • A picture related to the item with a frame around the image
  • A bulleted list of at least three reasons why the item is so awesome, using a font style, size, and color that enhances the overall design
  • A hyperlink to a website that relates to the item (this must be school-appropriate)
  • A footer with an original slogan for your item, using a font style, size, and color that enhances the overall design
  • No spelling errors
  • A properly formatted file name for the saved document: LastName_FirstName_Advertisement
