Word Basics

Why buy a car and a truck when you can buy an SUV (sport utility vehicle) and basically have both in one? Items that are versatile—think SUVs, smartphones, Swiss Army knives—maximize efficiency and productivity, which is important in this fast-paced world. Word Online is another mega multi-tasker. This one program has the muscle to create everything from basic letters and essays to stylish brochures, certificates, cover letters, flyers, resumes, and more.







After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • identify features of various word processing software
  • create and save a new Word document
  • identify different components of the Ribbon
  • utilize the Ribbon to perform tasks
  • modify text and paragraph settings
  • create bullet and numbered lists
  • find and replace content in a document
