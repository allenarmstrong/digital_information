Creating a Document

Documents are a breeze to create and access using Word Online.

Your name will appear in the top, right-hand corner when you are logged in to Word Online or any of the Office Online programs. You must be logged in to save your documents to the OneDrive.

Recent documents will appear on the left-hand side for easy access.

Select "New blank document" to create a new document from scratch.

Depending on your purpose, you can select a template that best fits your needs.
