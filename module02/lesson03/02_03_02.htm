Processing Your Words

Back in the day, writing something meant using a chisel and a slab of stone. And there was no "backspace." Luckily, technology has made writing way easier. Word processing software makes writing a report faster (no more messy handwriting to erase) and offers many features to enhance the document.

The Base Model

Just like a car, word processing programs come with standard features. Here are the most common:

  • modify text, line, and paragraph formatting
  • add page numbers
  • create tables, charts, and graphs
  • insert graphics
  • create bulleted and numbered lists
  • adjust page size, margins, and orientation
  • apply built-in templates
  • spell check documents
