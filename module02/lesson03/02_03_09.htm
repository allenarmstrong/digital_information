Give Me Some Space

Word Online gives plenty of options for making sure you create the spacing your teacher requires for that oh-so-important research paper. Let's explore. Type the following into a blank Word document:

Digital Information Technology is an exciting course. I, _________________, am going to learn all about Office Online during the _________ school year. I will start by learning Word Online. Word Online is a word processing software that I will use to write reports. Word Online will allow me to access my work anywhere I have connection to the internet. This means I can work on my report while at a friend's house and I don't even have to bring my own computer.

I will also learn how to use Excel Online. Excel Online is a spreadsheet software. Many businesses use Excel to help with analyzing their costs. People also use Excel at home to budget their household income.

Another great online tool that I will use is PowerPoint Online. PowerPoint Online is a presentation software that I will use to give presentations in class. Since my work will be saved in the cloud, I no longer have to worry about forgetting to bring my presentation to school because I will be able to access it from any computer that has internet connection.
