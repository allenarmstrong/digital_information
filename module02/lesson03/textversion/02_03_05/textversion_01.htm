The Ribbon—Text Version

  1. Undo allows you to undo or reverse your most recent action. If you change your mind, simply select "redo" and what you undid will reappear.
  2. Clipboard allows you to copy, cut, and paste text on the document. The format painter allows you to copy the formatting of text and apply it to text in other locations.
  3. Font allows you to change the way font is displayed onscreen. You can change the style, size, and color of a text. You can also bold, italicize, underline, strikethrough, subscript, superscript, and highlight text.
  4. Paragraph allows you to create bulleted and numbered lists; increase or decrease the indent of an entire paragraph; align text to the left, right, or center; and modify line spacing.
  5. Styles provide you with a variety of preformatted styles you can apply to your text.
  6. Editing allows you to search and replace words in a document.
