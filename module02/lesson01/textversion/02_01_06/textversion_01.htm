Using the Ribbon—Text Version

  • Slide One [image of Office Online ribbon]
    This section at the top of the document is called the Ribbon, which is comprised of three parts.
    1. Tabs represent an activity area.
    2. Groups show related items together under a tab.
    3. Commands are buttons or boxes where you enter the desired action.

  • Slide Two [image of Office Online ribbon, highlighting collapse arrow]
    You can hide the Ribbon by selecting the "collapse" arrow on the right side of the Ribbon.
