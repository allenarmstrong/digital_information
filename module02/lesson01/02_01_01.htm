Explore Office Online

Back in the day, if you needed to work with a partner or group on a project, you had to set a date, time, and a place to meet. In the workplace, that was often Monday through Friday, from 9–5, in some stuffy conference room. Luckily, those days are over. Now, computer technologies are making it easier to communicate, collaborate, and create—whenever and wherever.



After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • describe various computer technologies used for communication in the workplace
  • explain how features of business communication software improve the workplace
  • describe proper workspace ergonomics and its importance
  • explain basic keyboarding skills
  • identify and describe the purpose of the Office Online programs
  • create new files and folders, upload existing documents, and manage sharing properties
  • use the Ribbon to navigate tabs, groups, and commands
