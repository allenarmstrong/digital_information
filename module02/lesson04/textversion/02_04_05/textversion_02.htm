Header & Footer—Text Version

To create a header and footer in Word Online, begin by selecting "Header and Footer" on the "Insert" tab.

You can align your header and footer to the left, right, or center. Title the page with your header, and add a page number for your footer.

To see what it will look like on paper, you can check the "Reading View" under the "View" tab.

The header is now centered on every page, and the page numbers are located in the lower right corner.
