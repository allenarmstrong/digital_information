The Page Layout Ribbon—Text Version

  1. Page Setup allows you to adjust the margins (the borders, or edges, of the page; the computer defaults to one-inch borders on the left, right, top, and bottom of the page), the orientation (the position of the page), and the page size.
  2. Paragraph allows you to indent (move the beginning of a line or paragraph further from the margin) to the left or right and adjust the spacing between paragraphs.
