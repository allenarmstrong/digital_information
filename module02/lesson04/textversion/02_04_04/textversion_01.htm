Navigating the Insert Ribbon—Text Version

  1. The Page Break tab starts a new page at the current location in your document.
  2. The Table tab inserts or draws a table on your document.
  3. The Picture tab adds images saved on your computer to your document.
  4. The Online Pictures tab searches for images online and adds them to your document.
  5. The Link tab links your text to a website, a picture, a page, an email address, etc.
  6. The Comment tab adds notes to your document.
  7. The Header & Footer tab adds headers to the top of your document and footers to the bottom.
  8. The Page Numbers tab automatically adds page numbers to your document.
  9. The Insert Footnotes/Endnotes tabs add footnotes to text or endnotes to the end of your document.
  10. The Symbol tab adds various symbols to your document.
