Layouts and Design Theory

Think of a blank Word document as a fresh canvas, and you're the artist. Whether using a Word document to write a research paper for an English class, design a brochure for your club's car wash, or compose a business letter, it's your job to arrange the elements so they communicate your message clearly and in a visually appealing way.


After completing this lesson, you will be able to describe and apply:

  • page layout features
  • the Insert tab features
  • picture formatting features
  • table styles and features
  • hyperlink functions
  • references, footnotes, and endnotes
