How Does It Do That?

Have you ever stopped to think about how the internet takes you where you want to go? Most people take it for granted, but for curious minds like yours, this lesson will show you how the internet knows where to take you and how it gets you there in a snap.


After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • understand and apply level one Universal Resource Locator (URL) and associated protocols
  • identify types of internet networks and how they work
