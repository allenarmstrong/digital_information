While these books are awesome, they won't adequately prepare you for the workplace. Why? Most entry-level job texts (think computer documents, graphs, manuals, operating instructions, research material, formal correspondence, etc.) are written at the level of a college textbook (1200+L)! This means it is critical for you to practice reading texts that will truly prepare you for the workplace.
