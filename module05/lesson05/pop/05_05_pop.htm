05.05 Rock Your Research

Directions

For this assignment, you are going to take a blast to the past and research a previous decade. Do you want to learn more about the roaring 20s, the groovy 60s, or the awesome 80s? Now's your chance!

Step One: Select a Decade

Select a decade between 1800–2000.

Step Two: Download the Template

Download the Research Note-taking Template PDF or RTF.

Step Three: Do the Research

Use the internet to find information about three of the areas below as they were in your chosen decade. Make sure you use three different reliable sources.

Government Transportation
Food House and Furniture Design
Cost of Living Fashion
Hairstyles Occupations and Salaries
Contemporary Slang Social Rules and Customs
Three famous "firsts" of the decade Three major events of the decade
Entertainment: popular music, books, magazines, plays, movies, etc. Five people who lived and were famous for positive achievements during the decade

Step Four: Describe Your Decade

Use your research findings to summarize what you learned about your selected decade. Your description must be one paragraph in length, and you must give credit to your sources by using in-text citations. (See the examples below.) Add your summary to the bottom of your Research Note-taking Template.

In-Text Citation Examples:

  • According to (author name, website, organization), the most popular food of the 1970s was …
  • (Author name, website, organization, etc.) said the cost of living for 1980 averaged …
  • Warren G. Harding was the president from 1921-1923 but died of a heart attack (History.com).

Step Five: Save Your Work

Save your Research Note-taking Template and summary as LastName_FirstName_M5L5. If your name is Jane Smith, you would save the file as "Smith_Jane_M5L5."
