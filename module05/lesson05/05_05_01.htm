Rock Your Research

A long, long time ago (like in the 1990s), performing research meant going to the library, looking for sources in a card catalog, finding the resource material in the stacks, and then reading through the book to find information. Now, we have practically all the resources we need in one place: the internet. While the internet has made research easier, you still have to acquire the most relevant information and, more importantly, be able to comprehend it.


After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use various online reference materials
  • read and comprehend technical and non-technical resources
  • use the writing process for the creation of appropriate business documents
