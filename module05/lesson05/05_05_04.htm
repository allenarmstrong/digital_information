Take the Write Road

Imagine if you were going to take a trip. You wouldn't just get into the car (supposing you had a carâ€”oh, and a driver's license!) and start driving. You would decide on a destination, map your route, and pack what you need. Reading and researching are also better when you're prepared for the endeavor. A note-taking template can help you prepare for and organize your research by doing these things:

  • Record information by paraphrasing, summarizing, or using direct quotes from the text.
  • Collect available source information like author name, date of access, date of publication, page number, title, etc.
  • Process your thoughts about the information you're reading.
