Boolean Operators

Named after the 19th century English mathematician, George Boole


What are Boolean Operators?

Boolean Operators allow you to combine words and phrases using the words: AND, OR, NOT, and NEAR.


What does adding these Boolean Operators do?

Using an operator allows you to control what results come back to you from a search.


Today's Internet search engines default to Boolean search parameters, but it's good information to know.
