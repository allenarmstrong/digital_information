Fun Fact

According to Statista.com, here are some interesting facts about mobile apps and usage.

  • At the beginning of 2015, there were 1.4 million mobile apps available at the Apple App store.
  • In the fourth quarter of 2015, Facebook reported over 1.4 billion mobile users.
  • Americans spend an average of 3.40 hours on a mobile device, and 90% of that time is spent on mobile apps.
