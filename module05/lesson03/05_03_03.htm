A Whole New World

If you're curious to know how much money an NFL player makes each year, or why cats are afraid of cucumbers, or where Madagascar is located, the internet will have the answers. But if you want to count the calories you consume and tally the calories you expend every day, you are better off with a web or mobile app. When you download an app, you open up a whole new world.

Select each category to learn about different app uses and examples.
