The Life of the Internet

Even though there are over three billion internet users worldwide, many still have a lot to learn about the technology. Pop-quiz: What is an IP address? How do you clear your cache? Why do browsers need cross-platform compatibility? If your answers started with "um" or "uh," this lesson will provide those answers and more.



After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • demonstrate how to connect to the internet and use appropriate internet protocol
  • define key web terminology
  • identify internet, intranets, and extranets
  • define information technology terminology
